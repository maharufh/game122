Sample Question Paper - 2 2025-26

Time Allowed: 3 Hours      Maximum Marks: 80

General Instructions:

  1. All questions are compulsory.
  2. The question paper consists of 38 questions divided into 5 Sections A, B, C, D and E.
  3. Section A comprises of 20 questions of 1 mark each.
  4. Section B comprises of 5 questions of 2 marks each.
  5. Section C comprises of 6 questions of 3 marks each.
  6. Section D comprises of 4 questions of 5 marks each.
  7. Section E comprises of 3 case-based integrated units of assessment (4 marks each) with sub-parts.
  8. There is no overall choice. However, internal choices have been provided in some questions.

SECTION A

(20 Questions × 1 Mark = 20 Marks)

  1. The LCM of 12 and 18 is:
    (a) 36   (b) 72   (c) 24   (d) 48
  2. If α and β are zeroes of x² – 6x + 8, then α + β = ?
    (a) 6   (b) -6   (c) 8   (d) -8
  3. The pair of equations x + 2y = 4 and 2x + 4y = 8 has:
    (a) Unique solution   (b) No solution   (c) Infinitely many solutions   (d) Two solutions
  4. The 10th term of AP: -3, -1/2, 2, … is:
    (a) 21   (b) 23/2   (c) 19/2   (d) 25
  5. If sin θ = 3/5, then cos θ = ?
    (a) 4/5   (b) -4/5   (c) 3/5   (d) 5/4
  6. The perimeter of a sector with radius 10.5 cm and angle 120° is:
    (a) 36.5 cm   (b) 44 cm   (c) 55 cm   (d) 33 cm
  7. Volume of a sphere with radius 7 cm is:
    (a) 1372/3 cm³   (b) 4312/3 cm³   (c) 1437/3 cm³   (d) 154 cm³
  8. The median of first 10 prime numbers is:
    (a) 11   (b) 13   (c) 17   (d) 19
  9. A bag contains 5 red and 3 blue balls. Probability of drawing a red ball is:
    (a) 5/8   (b) 3/8   (c) 5/3   (d) 8/5
  10. The roots of x² + 5x + 6 = 0 are:
    (a) -2, -3   (b) 2, 3   (c) -1, -6   (d) 1, 6
  11. If tan θ = 1, then θ = ?
    (a) 30°   (b) 45°   (c) 60°   (d) 90°
  12. Area of triangle with sides 5 cm, 12 cm, 13 cm is:
    (a) 30 cm²   (b) 60 cm²   (c) 24 cm²   (d) 36 cm²
  13. The coordinates of midpoint of (3,4) and (7,10) are:
    (a) (5,7)   (b) (10,14)   (c) (2,3)   (d) (5,14)
  14. If two circles touch externally, distance between centres = ?
    (a) Sum of radii   (b) Difference of radii   (c) Product   (d) None
  15. The sum of probabilities of all events is:
    (a) 0   (b) 1   (c) 0.5   (d) ∞
  16. HCF(306, 657) = 9, then LCM(306, 657) = ?
    (a) 22338   (b) 22383   (c) 23338   (d) 23383
  17. Section formula divides join of (1,2) and (5,8) in 1:3 is:
    (a) (2, 3.5)   (b) (3,4)   (c) (4,5)   (d) (2,5)
  18. cos(90° - θ) = ?
    (a) sin θ   (b) cos θ   (c) tan θ   (d) cot θ
  19. Volume of hemisphere radius 6 cm is:
    (a) 144π cm³   (b) 288π cm³   (c) 72π cm³   (d) 216π cm³
  20. Assertion (A): 2 + √3 is irrational. Reason (R): Sum of rational and irrational is irrational.
    (a) Both true, R explains A   (b) Both true, R not explain   (c) A true R false   (d) A false

SECTION B

(5 Questions × 2 Marks = 10 Marks)

  1. Show that 3√2 is irrational.
  2. Find the zeroes of 4x² + 4x + 1.
  3. Solve: 3x – 4y = 10 and 2x + y = 7.
    OR Solve: x/2 + 2y/3 = 5, x + y = 9.
  4. Find the sum of first 25 terms of AP whose nth term is 3n + 4.
  5. If sin A = 3/5, find cos A and tan A.

SECTION C

(6 Questions × 3 Marks = 18 Marks)

  1. Prove that √2 + √3 is irrational.
  2. Find k if x² – 2kx + 7k – 12 = 0 has equal roots.
  3. A pole 6 m high casts shadow 2√3 m. Find angle of elevation of sun.
  4. Prove the identity: (sin θ + cos θ)² = 1 + sin 2θ.
  5. Find distance between points (-5,7) and (-1,3).
  6. The following data gives marks of students. Find median.
    Marks0-1010-2020-3030-4040-50
    No. of students81220155

SECTION D

(4 Questions × 5 Marks = 20 Marks)

  1. A boat goes 30 km upstream and 44 km downstream in 10 hours. In 13 hours, it goes 40 km upstream and 55 km downstream. Find speed of boat and stream.
  2. Draw less than ogive for the given data and find median graphically.
    (Assume suitable grouped data table in question)
  3. Construct a pair of tangents to a circle of radius 4 cm from a point 10 cm away.
    OR Construct ΔABC with BC=7 cm, ∠B=60°, AB+AC=12 cm.
  4. A metallic sphere of radius 4.2 cm is melted and recast into a cylinder of radius 6 cm. Find height of cylinder.

SECTION E

(3 Case-Based Questions × 4 Marks = 12 Marks)

36. Age Word Problem (Quadratic Equations)
The present age of a father is three times the age of his son. After 5 years, father's age will be two times the son's age.

(i) Form the quadratic equation for son's present age. (1)
(ii) Find the present ages of son and father. (1)
(iii) Find their ages 10 years ago. (1)
(iv) State nature of roots and verify. (1)
37. Shadow of Tower (Trigonometry + Coordinate)
A tower stands at point T(0,0). A person at point P(100,0) observes top of tower at 30° elevation. Another person at Q(200,0) observes at 45° elevation.

(i) Find height of tower using point P. (1)
(ii) Verify using point Q. (1)
(iii) Find distance from P to base if angle was 45°. (1)
(iv) Find coordinates of top of tower. (1)
38. Rainfall Data (Statistics + Probability)
Monthly rainfall (in cm) in a city for 12 months:
3, 5, 0, 2, 8, 10, 12, 15, 4, 1, 0, 6

(i) Find mean rainfall. (1)
(ii) Find median rainfall. (1)
(iii) Find mode. (1)
(iv) Probability that rainfall in a random month exceeds 10 cm. (1)
